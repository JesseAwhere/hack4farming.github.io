Hack4Farming is Building Technology Bridges

Agriculture organizations around the world are working hard to help smallholder farmers adapt to changing conditions and take advantage of new technologies. But these organizations face numerous challenges in collecting, managing, and communicating their knowledge and data effectively. Hack4Farming hackathons team agriculture experts with local technical talent in an intensive, short-duration event to coordinate and create technology solutions for farming. Each Hack4Farming event is unique in the group of experts and techies it pulls together and the challenge it seeks to address. Our outlook is global in nature, and we continue to expand our hackathons to new regions. Hack4Farming is a project of aWhere, Inc.
