Ready to Hack with Us?

Hack4Farming is Building Technology Bridges

Farmers, value chain actors, and agricultural development researchers around the world are working hard to help smallholders adapt to changing conditions. But these organizations face numerous challenges in collecting data and communicating knowledge.

Hack4Farming hackathons team agriculture experts with local technical talent in the places where high-resolution weather data is most needed. Events are intensive and collaborative - participants share experience and skills to brainstorm new ideas, new business plans, and new technology solutions for farming. Each Hack4Farming event is unique in the group of experts and techies it pulls together and the challenge it seeks to address.

We believe the opportunity in farming is great, and the potential applications are endless. Our outlook is global in nature, and we continue to expand our hackathons to new regions.
